About Midlothian

Just twenty minutes south of the capital city of Richmond, lies the quaint town of Midlothian.
A home to many families, communities, and bustling businesses, It's a great place to be able to
call home. Those who live here get a taste of the simple suburban lifestyle, but don't let that fool
you. There's always something to do, whether it be shopping at the more populated and touristy areas,
or hunting in the more rural locations. This town has a truly fantastic mix of everything, and is perfect for any
kind of person!

A rich history

Anyone living in Midlothian could tell you the cultural history of the town, because It's quite
hard to miss. Along the local streets, nature hikes, and even inside beside the schools, coal mines can be spotted.
They aren't in use anymore, but long ago they once they paved the way for our great town. It all began in the 1800s,
when the Woodridge family began their coal mining enterprise. The whole county of Chesterfield became
a hot spot for mining. Wikipedia says "Midlothian is the site of the first commercially-mined coal in
the Colony of Virginia and North America." How cool is that?

Size, and other information

The great town of Midlothian is approximately 5.48 miles wide, and is a home to 72,711 amazing people
and 26,206 households. The zip codes are 23113 and 23114, and it resides inside Chesterfield County.


The racial demographics are 73.3% white, 15.7% African American, 4.8% Hispanic, 3.5% Asian, 2.9% mixed
races, and 0.2% Native American.

Attractions

When looking for a fun time, the townspeople:

  • Walk along the Mid-Lothian Mines park
  • Swim in the lake and explore the Robious Landing park
  • Play mini golf and drive-in golf at the Windy Hill Sports Complex
  • Go antique shopping
  • Bowl and play arcade games at Uptown Alley
  • Visit historical landmarks

And much more!
