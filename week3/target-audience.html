The target audience for this site are people in the Midlothian, VA community
who want to get in contact with local businesses. This includes those who are
job hunting, networking, or just curious of what the town has to offer.
On a broader scale, young adults to seniors who are looking for opportunities.

Persona 1: Kelly. Kelly is a newly wedded wife, looking for job opportunities.
she currenly doesn't have a flow of steady income, and is trying to change that.
Newly graduated from college with a degree in Communications, she needs help finding
a reasonable career. She finds this website during a quick google search of
"Jobs in my area hiring".

Persona 2: Clark. Clark is an older single man of 50 years old. He has served his entire
adult life as an accountant, and makes an average salary of 50k a year. He earned an accounting
degree 30 years back. He finds this website through advertisements posted in the local library

  1. What can I do in Midlothian with a communications degree?
  2. When and where is the nearest hiring event?
  3. How should I prepare for an interveiw?
  4. What does the general economy look like in town?
  5. How do the business opportunities look compared to other towns?
