Frequently Asked Questions

Curious about something? Chances are, your question has already been asked.

Do I need to have anything ready before the appointment?

No. Ryann will bring everything needed including towels, products, and anything else. The only thing to worry about is having a chair set up for you to sit in.

Are you licensed to do this?

Ryann has been a lisenced professional for 21 years. Some states require special licensing to provide mobile services, but Virginia does not. Ryann has a business license and that is all that is required.

How do you wash the color out of my hair?

All hair is washed in the sink using a spray nozzle. Old school style, the way your grandma used to wash her hair as a kid… In the kitchen sink! It’s affective and fast.

How do I know your tools are sanitized between each client?

I bring sanitizer solution to your home and sanitize each implement in front of you before I use it. Each client gets a fresh set of towels and a cape. I do not reuse these on clients, they all get washed at the end of the day.

How do you take payment?

I accept PayPal, Venmo, Zell, Apple Pay, cash or check.

Will you come to my house for any service?

I have a $50 minimun per visit.
